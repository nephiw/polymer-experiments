Polymer Experiments

These are a series of simple experiments that I built while working to understand how to use Polymer. They are intentionally very simple and they work up through the documentation on the getting started site and on the quick start tour

  • Registering a Polymer Element
  • Working with the local DOM
  • Composing DOM from content
  • Binding to a local property
  • Binding to a public property
  • Two directional binding
  • A complete toggle button
